2024 winner:

Better Days
❤ ❤ ❤ ❤ ❤

Dil Se: Here comes 2nd movie of our top 5 movies in the 2024. Amar and Meghna. Dil Se movie portrays story between two hearts, where out of two one fought for togetherness while the other did everything just to fight the love it felt. Love grew over the course of few meetings, but at the end that same love ruined them both.

❤ ❤ ❤ ❤ ❤

20th Century Girl: A romantic tale of love and betrayal, or was it betrayal? Welcome to the world of k-trauma as korean movie and drama fans love to call those bittersweet shows. This movie will either break you or make you, no in between, as it showcases in the best way possible how we can never know what is truly going on around us.

❤ ❤ ❤ ❤ ❤

Sanam Re: You know those movies that leave you feeling empty for a while, but still so happy as you have witnessed love that deep? Yeah this it that one. Tighten your seatbelts is all that I am gonna say as the end will leave you in deep haze of wonder, sadness, shock and intrigue.

❤ ❤ ❤ ❤ ❤

Ten years in love: Just as you think it is the end, there they come again. They walk through that door and then as nothing has ever changed it all clicks again. Story about two people, one unlucky woman and a doctor she randomly meets one day, never knowing even in her wildest dreams that they are gonna be intertwined forever...
