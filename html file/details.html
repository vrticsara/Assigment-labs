Hello to all of my new followers.

I am Sara Vrtić, the creator of Sara's Movie Blog. I thought for a while about my project topic and what am I going to do about all of this, but at the end my heart decided on doing a little something which will showcase my love towards Asian culture, in general. I made this website as to share my favorite movies, exchange recommendations and show the world beauty of Asian movies in hopes of meeting more movie enthusiasts like me. When I'm not watching movies, I enjoy exploring different cultures, reading about supernatural stuff, and experimenting with web design. I want to thank you for awesome recommendations, thank you for voting and in general for great support. Whenever you vote and follow me it helps a lot with reaching bigger audience.

I also wanted to add how my about page is constantly changing video intro as requested from other users in the system, but every end of the year for two months, both November and December, I put up my own favorite movie of that year. For this 2024 year, it is the Hindi movie Dil Se which showcases deep and unexpected love between Amar and Meghna, two people who never got to be properly together, but the whole time since they met each other and until their demise, they only wanted one another.

My mission is to bring together movie lovers from around the world and build a community that appreciates the beauty of storytelling in cinema. Through this blog, I hope to introduce you to incredible movies that you might not have encountered before as well as discover many more which I never knew about, through you. My specific field and movies which I prefer watching are as shown: Hindi, Chinese, and Korean.

Feel free to send whatever kind of movie as all are up for discussion at least privately if not here on this website.
